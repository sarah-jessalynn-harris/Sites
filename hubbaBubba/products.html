Bubble Blast

The latest edition to the brand, HUBBA BUBBA Bubble Blast chewing gum is a perfect example of what bubble gum should be. Each round piece provides plenty of gum for blowing magnificent bubbles! Try your hand at blowing your bubble record, or hold a contest with friends!

Bubble Jug

HUBBA BUBBA Bubble Jug is a bottle of fun! This retro gum, with its tropical theme, was popular in the 90's, and has since disappeared from shelves. If this jug ever makes a comeback, be sure to "shake 'n' enjoy" this sand-inspired, powdered gum to your hearts content!

Hubba Bubba Max

Based off an original classic, HUBBA BUBBA Max is by far packed with more flavor than any other gum. These chunky pieces have two layers of flavor, giving you a taste for hours to come.

Ouch!

HUBBA BUBBA Ouch! can cure any boo-boo in a flash! While these band-aid-shaped treats are not for ouchies, you can chew them with health in mind: they are sugar free!

Bubble Tape

HUBBA BUBBA Bubble Tape goes the length to bring you gum entertainment! With a debut in 1998 and a comeback in 2003, this tape has brought joy to people of all ages! "It's six feet of gum- for you, not them", but honestly, share if you want to. There's plenty to go around!
