Chew on this!

Ready to hear about the fascinating past of HUBBA BUBBA? Here is a short timeline of some of the brand's most significant times. More information can be found here , as well.

1979

Hubba Bubba was the first bubble gum introduced by the Wrigley Company. The name comes from the phrase "Hubba Hubba" which was used by soldiers during World War II to express approval.

1980's

The Wrigley Company decided to exit the U.S. bubble gum business temporarily and ceased production of Hubba Bubba chunks.

1980's

The Wrigley Company decided to exit the U.S. bubble gum business temporarily and ceased production of Hubba Bubba chunks. Focus was shifted to Britain.

2003 - 2004

Wrigley acquires Bubble Tape and Squeeze Pop. Hubba Bubba chunks made their return with the launch of Hubba Bubba Max gum - a unique and captivating product with extraordinarily long lasting flavor and the combinations of two colors and two flavors in one chunk.

2007

Hubba Bubba Sour Gummi Tape, six feet of gummi candy in a clam shell package, launched in the U.S.

2008

Wrigley launched Hubba Bubba Glop, a unique candy-coated, liquid filled bubblegum that explodes with flavors and comes in a unique pop-top tube.

2009

Hubba Bubba Max introduces Sweet & Sassy Cherry. The classic band-aid gum Hubba Bubba Ouch! re-launches in October to provide a sugar-free bubble gum option, available in Slim Pack. Each pack comes with one of 20 different available games.

Burst your bubble yet?

Now that you've engaged in the intriguing past of HUBBA BUBBA, are you ready for some more recent history? Here is a short timeline of some of the brand's more recent times. More information can be found here , too.

2010

In 2010 Hubba Bubba released Mystery Max and Mystery Tape, a great-tasting and unique mystery flavor that is sure to leave gum chewers guessing.

2012

Hubba Bubba partners with Dr Peppe Snapple Group to launch new flavors of bubble gum chunk in new and exciting flavors, Dr. Pepper and Orange Crush.

2013

Hubba Bubba launches new flavors of bubble gum chunk with Dr Pepper Snapple Group in new and exciting flavors, A&W Root Beer and Hawaiian Punch.

2015

Hubba Bubba launches new flavors of bubble gum chunk with Dr Pepper Snapple Group in new and exciting flavors, Crush Grape and Dr Pepper Cherry.
