Meet Jacob

Hi There! My name is Jacob -

- And I love playing tuba! I started with it in middle school and never stopped. I recently graduated from Indiana University Bloomington's Jacobs School of Music with a Bachelor's in Music for tuba performance. Even though my academic career is complete, I'm always looking for more opportunities to play music.

I like to perform all types of music from all over the world. I love learning about all kinds of instruments, from the niche edges of modern music culture to the traditional instruments found around the globe.

These days, I am active in the Martinsville Community Band that I first joined when I was in middle school. Every Christmas, I attend at least one local Tuba Christmas to celebrate the season. I often practice in local parks and invite my friends over for a good jam session. I have started uploading bi-weekly tuba videos on my Instagram to show what different types of music can be played on tuba.

Outside of music, I enjoy retro video games, drawing, and spending time with my close family and friends. I love to go out in nature and experience the outdoors (which is why you'll see many of my recordings are done outside!) I currently live in Waverly, IN with my lovely wife who made this awesome site for me!

I'm interested in fun music opportunities near centeral Indiana, and connecting with more musicians locally and globally. I'd love to hear from you!
