Adverbs

"How Much" "When" "Where" "How" Emphasis

Use of Adverbs

Adverbs modify verbs, other adverbs, and adjectives; furthermore, they indicate quantity, time, place, manner, and intensity. ¹ They do not change in gender or number.

"How Much"

Quantity/degree adverbs answer the question “how much”. ²

ex: She runs a lot.

Ella corre mucho.

ex: She runs badly.

Ella corre mal.

"When"

Time adverbs answer the question “when”. ³

ex: I am late.

Llego tarde.

ex: She will come soon.

Llegará pronto.

"Where"

Place adverbs answer the question “where” . ⁴

ex: We looked there.

Vemos allí.

ex: We looked over there.

Vemos acá.

"How"

Manner adverbs answer the question “how”. ⁵

ex: Habla rápidamente.

She speaks quickly.

Emphasis

Intensity adverbs are used for emphasis. ⁶

ex: She seriously wants to learn another language.

Quiere aprender otra lengua en serio.
