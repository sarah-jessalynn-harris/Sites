Use of Preterite Anterior

The preterite anterior is the preterite form of haber and a past participle. This tense is not really used anymore, and it is found mostly in literature. The preterite anterior is used in the same way as the pluperfect; however, the preterite anterior occurs when there is no time difference between one action and another.
