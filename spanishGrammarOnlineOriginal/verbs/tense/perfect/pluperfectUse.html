Pluperfect

Pluperfect Uses Examples Conjugation

Use of Pluperfect

The pluperfect is used as translated: had ____ed. The action occurs in the past before another action takes place.¹ Haber means “to have”. Pluperfect is when you conjugate haber in the imperfect, making it “had”. Adding the past participle is the “___ed”.

Examples

I had eaten in this restaurant before.

Había comido en este restaurante antes.

She has been running a lot this week.

Había estado corriendo mucho este semana.

I had completed the project before going out.

Había cumplido el proyecto antes de salir.

They had gone to the market.

Habían ido al mercado.
