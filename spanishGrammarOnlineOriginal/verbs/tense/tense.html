Introduction to the 14 Spanish Tenses

Tense lets us know when an action takes place. In Spanish, there are 14 different tenses that can be used. 7 of theses tenses are known as the simple tenses and the other 7 are called the perfect tenses or the compound tenses, as they require two parts to be conjugated. 4 of the 14 tenses are specifically used in the subjunctive mood, whilst the rest are to be used in the indicative. Explore the tenses and their types below.
