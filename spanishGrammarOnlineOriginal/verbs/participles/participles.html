Introduction to Participles

Participles are impersonal forms of verbs. They are often used in conjunction with auxiliary verbs and can sometimes be used as adjectives or adverbs. There are two types of participles in Spanish: the present participle, also known as a gerund, and the past participle. Each one has its own special uses. You can learn more about these forms by exploring the categories below.
