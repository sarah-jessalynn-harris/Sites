Definite Indefinite

Introduction to Articles

Just as in English, the language Spanish uses definite and indefinite articles. As always, these articles must agree in gender and number with the noun.

Definite

There is only one definite article in English: the. In Spanish there are four, to account for gender and number agreement: el, la, los, las.

el is used for singular, masculine nouns

la is used for singular, feminine nouns

los is used for plural, masculine nouns

las is used for plural, feminine nouns

Indefinite

There are only three indefinite articles in English, two for singular nouns, a or an, and one for plural nouns, some. In Spanish there are four, to account for gender and number agreement: un, una, unos, unas.

un is used for singular, masculine nouns

una is used for singular, feminine nouns

unos is used for plural, masculine nouns

unas is used for plural, feminine nouns
