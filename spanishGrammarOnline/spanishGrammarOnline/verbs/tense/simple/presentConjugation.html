Present Tense Conjugation

When conjugating verbs, there are rules for each tense. There are certain forms for each tense and for each subject, based off of the original infinitive. There are regular rules; however, every tense has its irregularities. Below you can find out how to conjugate the present tense in Spanish.

Regular Conjugation

This is how you conjugate verbs regularly in the presesnt tense. You drop the ending of the infinitive and add the following endings.

Irregular Conjugation

Verbs often have irregular forms within each tense. Below you can find the different types of irregular verb conjugations for the present tense of the indicative mood.

Stem Changing/Boot Verbs

Certain verbs have changes to the root or the stem of the infinitive when conjugated. This change is only applied to the 1st person singular (yo), 2nd person singular (tú), 3rd person singular (él), and 3rd person plural (ellos) forms. When forming a conjugation chart, if you circle the forms that change, a boot shape is formed, as shown below.

There are distinct types of stem changing verbs. All of this type change a vowel to a different vowel or a combination of two. The groups are:

e to i (-ir)

pedir
pido pedimos
pides pedís
pide piden

e to ie (-ar, -er, -ir)

cerrar
cierro cerramos
cierras cerráis
cierra cierran
perder
pierdo perdemos
pierdes perdéis
pierde pierden
mentir
miento mentimos
mientes mentís
miente mienten

i to í (-ar)

enviar
envío enviamos
envías enviáis
envía envían

i to y (-ir) *For verbs ending in –uir (but not –guir)*

huir
huyo huimos
huyes huís
huye huyen

o to ue (-ar, -er, -ir)

mostrar
muestro mostramos
muestras mostráis
muestra muestran
poder

dormir

u to ú (-ar)

Spelling Changing

Some verbs have spelling changes when they are conjugated as to keep the pronunciation.

When there is a vowel in front of the c, the yo form adds a z before the c

conocer
conozco conocemos
conoces conocéis
conoce conocen
lucir
luzco lucimos
luces lucís
luce lucen

When a consonant is in front of the c, the yo form replaces the c with z

vencer
venzo vencemos
vences vencéis
vence vencen
zurcir
zurzo zurcimos
zurces zurcís
zurce zurcen

–ger and –gir

Change the g in the yo form to j to preserve the “h” sound.

(otherwise it is a “go” sound)

escoger
escojo escogemos
escoges escogéis
escoge escogen
exigir
exijo exigimos
exiges exigís
exige exigen

–guir

Change the gu to g for the yo form

(to maintain the “go” sound)

distinguir
distingo distinguimos
distingues distinguís
distingue distinguen
