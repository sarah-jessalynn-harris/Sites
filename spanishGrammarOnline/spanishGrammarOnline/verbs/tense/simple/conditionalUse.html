Conditional Tense Uses

Verbs are used to describe actions. Tense describes when these actions take place. The conditional tense of the indicative mood refers actions that would have happened in the past, given a set of conditions. It can also have other contexts, as can be seen below.

Possible Past Events

The conditional is used to express what would have happened if something else had not happened in the past ¹

ex: Filipe would go dancing but he doesn't have dancing shoes.

Filipe iría a bailar pero no tiene zapatos de baile.

"If" Phrases

The conditional is used with conditional frases using “if” and the past subjunctive.

ex: I would get married if someone were in love with me.

Me casaría si alguien estuviera enamorado de mí.

ex: We would sing with you all if we were able to.

Cantaríamos con vosotros si pudiéramos.

Probability/Conjecture

The conditional can be utilized to express wonderment or probability in the past ²

ex: Where could Ana have been?

¿Dónde estaría Ana?

ex: She must have been at home.

Estaría en casa.

ex: I wondered when she was coming.

¿Cuándo vendría ella?

ex: She probably arrived at 6.

Llegaría a las seis.

ex: I wonder what time it was when she left.

¿Qué hora sería cuando salió?

"Future of the Past"

The conditional can be seen as the future of the past. The relationship between the past tenses and the conditional is similar to the relationship between the present and the future. ⁴

ex: They said that they would play.

Dijeron que jugarían.

Compare the sentance above, which uses the preterite and the

conditional to the sentence below, which uses the present and

the future.

ex: They say that they will play.

Dicen que jugarán.

Courtesy

Often, the preterite is used to describe actions that interrupt other actions that were in progress. The actions that are already in progress will be described with the imperfect.

ex: Would you like tea?

¿Querrías té?

*instead of: Do you want tea?

¿Quieres té?

ex: She ought to go home.

Debería ir a casa.

*instead of: She should go home.

Debe ir a casa.

ex: Would you be able to make me dinner?

¿Podrías prepararme la cena?

*instead of: Can you make me some dinner?

¿Puedes prepararme la cena?
