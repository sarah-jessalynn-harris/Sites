Present Perfect Conjugation

When conjugating verbs, there are rules for each tense. There are certain forms for each tense and for each subject, based off of the original infinitive. There are regular rules; however, every tense has its irregularities. Below you can find out how to conjugate the present perfect in Spanish.

Regular Conjugation

When conjugating perfect tenses, you use a form of haber and the past participle of your selected verb. The present perfect uses the present forms of haber.

Pronouns
yo nosotros
tú vosotros
él ellos
Haber
he hemos
has habéis
ha han

Irregular Conjugation

The only irregularites for perfect tenses are the past participles. Memorize the odd ones so that you know which are irregular. You can also visit this page for more information about past participles.
