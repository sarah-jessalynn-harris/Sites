Practice Makes Perfect

Below are some different activities to practice your Spanish grammar skills. The first type of activity is practice with articles. The second activity works with mood. The final activity is practice conjugating verbs in each tense. The more you practice and use the language, the better you will become.

Article Practice

Practice with articles, "the" and "a" in English. Use the buttons to select the correct definite article (el, la, los, las) or the correct indefinite article (un, una, unos, unas) for each noun shown.

Mood Practice

For this practice, you must choose from the multiple choice options to match each verb shown with its tense and mood. This is a great way to learn the names of different tenses and see how the look in different moods.
