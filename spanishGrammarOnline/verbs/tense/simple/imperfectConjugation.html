Imperfect Tense Conjugation

When conjugating verbs, there are rules for each tense. There are certain forms for each tense and for each subject, based off of the original infinitive. There are regular rules; however, every tense has its irregularities. Below you can find out how to conjugate the imperfect tense in Spanish.

Regular Conjugation

This is how you conjugate verbs regularly in the imperfect tense. You drop the ending of the infinitive and add the following endings.

Irregular Conjugation

Verbs often have irregular forms within each tense. Below you can find the 3 different types of irregular verb conjugations for the imperfect tense of the indicative mood.

Ser/Ir/Ver

There are only 3 irregular verb conjugation for the imperfect. Ser, ir, and ver are shown conjugated irregularly in the imperfect below.
