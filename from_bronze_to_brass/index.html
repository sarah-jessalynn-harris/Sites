About Bronze to Brass

From Bronze to Brass offers tutoring to students of brass from Jacob Kopis, a tubist at the Jacobs School of Music at Indiana University. He is dedicated to helping young muscisians better their musical skills so that they may pursue their dreams in the music sphere.

This site is here for you to learn more about these lessons and sign up for them. Explore the topics below to find out what lesson plans work for you.

Lessons

Lessons

From Bronze to Brass offers two types of tutoring sessions, based on duration of time. One Hour and Half Hour sessions are available, each with different techniques used and prices charged.

Half Hour Sessions

Half hour sessions consist of reviewing a piece of music or going over one musical topic. Each 30 minute session costs $10.

One Hour Sessions

One hour sessions consist of beginning with a musical topic followed by reviewing a few pieces of music. Each 1 hour session costs $20.

Schedules

Scheduling

From Bronze to Brass offers four categories of schedules for the tutoring sessions. There are schedules designed to help you improve your skills based on your level of experience. These schedules are simply suggestions if you are unsure of what you may need in terms of lessons. Alternatively, you can have a “Freeform” schedule that changes week to week. Learn more by exploring the descriptions below.

Beginner

Beginners can take this lesson course designed to help them develop their foundational skills in a timely manner. This schedule consists of one 1 hour session per week, a cost of $20 per week or $80 per month.

Intermediate

Intermediates can take this lesson course designed to help them develop their existing skills in a timely manner. This schedule consists of one 1 hour session bi-weekly, a cost of $20 per session or $40 a month.

Advanced

The Advanced lesson course designed to help them hone their experience and skills over time. This schedule consists of one 30 minute sessions bi-weekly, a cost of $10 per session or $20 per month.

Freeform

If you are unable to work with the other scheduling types, or you simply need a more personalized schedule, you can opt to participate the Freeform scheduling. The prices vary as you may choose the lesson type and how often you would like to schedule them.
