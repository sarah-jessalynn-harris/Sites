About

Sarah Jessalynn Harris is an aspiring front-end web developer from central Indiana. She is currently proficient in HTML, CSS, Sass, PHP, React.js and JavaScript. She has also had experience with WordPress and custom themes. She is one of two webmasters for IUPUI's joint honors societies of Alpha Lambda Delta and Phi Eta Sigma for the 2018-2019 school year. She is gaining a lot of experience through her studies at IUPUI's School of Informatics and Computing(SOIC), where she is working to recieve her Bachelor's degree in Media Arts and Science with a Specialization in Web Design and Development. She is also minoring in Informatics at SOIC and Computer and Information Technology at the Purdue School of Engineering. She hopes to work with a company local to Indiana creating websites and web applications after graduating.

"I want to be a developer who makes websites with logic, artistry, and love."

Sarah feels that web development and design is a great fit for her, because it complements many positive traits in her personality. She loves to be creative, and enjoys making beautiful things. Web development is an art, and the design process is not always an easy task. The challenges that both designing and developing bring to the table really allow her to use her critical thinking skills, and she thoroughly enjoys the challenge. But more than all this, Sarah loves the fact that she can help others achieve their dreams by being a web developer.

Sarah also enjoys many activities outside the field of the net. Her favorite pastimes include cooking, drawing, gaming, reading, writing, and spending time with family and friends. She also is working to become fluent in Spanish and has been studying the language for 6 years. For her work in Spanish she received a Certificate of Multilingual Proficiency from the State of Indiana in 2017.
